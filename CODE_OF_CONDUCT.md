# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We aim for a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and considerate.
- Give and accept feedback in good faith.
- Focus discussion on improving BrevityPrompt.
- Respect privacy. Do not share another person's private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, abusive language, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Sexualized content or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and
other interactions that violate this Code. Report concerns privately to a
maintainer; reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially where
possible.

## Scope

This Code applies to project spaces and to public conduct when representing
BrevityPrompt.
